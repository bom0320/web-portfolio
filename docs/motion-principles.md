# Motion Principles

## Philosophy

Motion is not decoration.

Motion is part of the information architecture.

---

## Scroll Feel

- smooth acceleration
- cinematic deceleration
- natural settling
- avoid abrupt snapping

---

## Transition Rules

- scenes should feel connected
- motion should guide attention
- transitions may overlap slightly
- avoid isolated animations

---

## Interaction Direction

The portfolio should feel:

- cinematic
- immersive
- narrative-driven
- interaction-oriented
